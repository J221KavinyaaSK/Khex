The plot follows an investigation carried out by 
17-year old true crime enthusiast Pippa, a high 
school student in the fictional town of 
Little Kilton.

Theme: A Good Girl's Guide to Murder is a mystery book full of plot twists and red herrings with themes of family, courage and perseverance. The story is told through the eyes of Pippa, a teenager with a mind that takes an intriguing perspective on life.
